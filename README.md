# 🛒 Superstore Sales Analysis Dashboard

An interactive, business-intelligence style Excel dashboard engineered to analyze revenue efficiency, seasonal profit margins, product sub-category performance, and consumer segment distributions for a retail superstore. 

This project demonstrates advanced data visualization, trend line modeling, and multi-variable pivot reporting using Microsoft Excel.

---

### 🎯 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) Captured
* **Total Sales Volume:** $35,449.52
* **Total Profit Generated:** $1,270.69
* **Total Transactions/Orders:** 69
* **Peak Performance Window:** June–July (Highest Profit & Sales Months)

---

### 💡 Data Insights & Metrics
* **Seasonal Sales & Profit Trends:** While sales peaked heavily in **July ($18,710.44)**, profit optimization peaked alongside it ($976.44), showing strong operational health during summer months. A second sales spike occurred in **November ($14,443.39)**.
* **Customer Segment Contribution:** The market share is highly balanced, with **Corporate (34%)** and **Home Office (34%)** segments barely edging out standard **Consumers (32%)**.
* **Geographic Order Volume:** The **West** region handles the highest volume of fulfillment (194 Corporate orders, 162 Home Office, and 115 Consumer orders). 
* **Product Category Leadership:** **Phones** (Technology) generated the highest independent sales volume at **$21,815.53** yielding $1,116.28 in profit. 
* **Profit Drainage:** The **Binders** sub-category under Office Supplies represents a leak in the margin, running at a net loss of **-$89.22** despite over $16.9K in sales.

---

### 🛠️ Features & Tools Used
* **Advanced Chart Aggregation:** Combined dynamic dual-axis charts (Sales vs. Profit Over Time) integrated with linear trend lines for forecasting profit trajectories.
* **Multi-Layered Visual Layouts:** Leveraged donor charts for percentage distributions, stacked columns for regional order splits, and nested sub-category breakdowns.
* **Interactive Filtering Slicers:** Embedded seamless slice panels filtering globally across *Customer Segment*, *Category*, and *Geographical Region*.
* **Design Philosophy:** Clean, professional corporate orange theme with custom KPI callout blocks to immediately highlight macro health metrics.
